All Sacred Texts

Discovering the shared wisdom of humanity through AI and scripture.

Mission: To illuminate the spiritual and historical connections that unite humanity by leveraging the power of open-source AI to explore sacred texts across cultures, eras, and faiths — fostering curiosity, reverence, and discovery for the betterment of all.

We believe the wisdom of the past still holds truths yet to be uncovered.

  • Make ancient scriptures and religious texts from every tradition accessible, comparable, and searchable.
  • Invite individuals of all backgrounds to participate in respectful exploration and interfaith learning.
  • Develop open AI interfaces that reveal patterns, parallels, and hidden connections that might otherwise remain buried.
  • Champion collaboration, scholarship, and discovery as shared values in a new era of understanding.

This is a project built in the spirit of good faith — not to promote any one religion, but to honor all. It is an invitation — to scholars, seekers, skeptics, and spiritualists alike — to discover what unites us.
