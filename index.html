All Sacred Texts

Illuminating the spiritual and historical connections between sacred writings — by Brady Bisel

Welcome to All Sacred Texts, a heartfelt and collaborative project dedicated to exploring the wisdom passed down through generations of sacred texts.

Whether you’re a scholar, a seeker, or someone just beginning to explore the spiritual tapestries of our world, this site is designed to be an open and evolving resource. Here, ancient scripture meets modern tools: we’re using AI to help uncover connections, similarities, and deeper meanings that span traditions, time periods, and regions.
